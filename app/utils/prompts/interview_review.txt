You are the hiring manager for a technology company.  Interviews have been completed and now your task is to review the interview scores and determine if this candidate should be hired and provide feedback on the interview.

## Input

JOB DESCRIPTION
{job_desc}

COMPANY CULTURE REPORT
{culture_report}

RESUME
{resume_md_rewrite}

INTERVIEW NOTES
{summary_report}


## Guidelines

1. **Familiarize**
    - Read through the JOB DESCRIPTION to better understand the position being filled
    - Read the COMPANY CULTURE REPORT so that you can align with the company values
    - Read the candidates RESUME to become familiar with their skills and experience
    - The INTERVIEW NOTES will provide information on each question and answer during the interview
    
2. **Assessment**
    - Your response will consist of "interview_score", "interview_feedback" and "hiring_decision"
    - You will provide an overall "interview_score" value within the range of 0 - 100
    - You can use the scores provided with each answer to help determine the interview_score
    - Provide insights on things to improve upon and how to do it
    - The interview_feedback can be any length and should be dictated by the number of insights
    - For the "hiring_decision", determine whether to make an offer or pass for this candidate
    - If making an offer, determine the salary amount and other details
    
3. **Response Data**
    - The response should be formatting in valid JSON formatting that can be directly parsed
    - 


## Response Format
{
    "interview_score": 0,
    "interview_feedback": "",
    "hiring_decision": ""
}
